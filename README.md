# 🖱️ Click Carefully

## What is this?

**Click Carefully** is a cybersecurity awareness game where you play as an executive assistant to the CEO of a mid-sized tech company.

Your job? **Filter the CEO's inbox.** Decide which emails are safe and which are phishing attempts. Every correct decision earns company money. Every mistake costs you.

You'll play through **5 in-game days (Monday to Friday)**. Each day includes a **lunch break random event** that tests physical security habits (locking your screen, spotting tailgaters, etc.).

## Who is this for?

- Employees who need security awareness training  
- Anyone who wants to get better at spotting phishing emails  
- No technical background required  

## How to play

1. Read each email (sender, subject, body)  
2. Click **Safe** or **Phish**  
3. Learn from feedback after every choice  
4. Complete lunch break scenarios   

## Core features

- **5 progressive days** (Monday = basics, Friday = advanced attacks)  
- **Realistic email examples** (typos, lookalike domains, urgency tricks)  
- **Lunch break mini-games** (physical and policy security)  
- **Immediate feedback** with explanations  
- **Scoring system** (gain or lose virtual money)

## Tech stack

- **Engine:** Godot 4.6  
- **Language:** GDScript  
- **Platform:** PC / Web export

## Project status

Prototype stage. One fully playable week (5 days + lunch events) targeting employees in progress.
